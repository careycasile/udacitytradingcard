Orca

The average adult Orca weighs approximately 8,000 pounds.

  • Habitat: Ocean
  • Life Span: 20-30 years
  • Behavior: Travel in packs
  • Food Source: Smaller aquatic creatures

The killer whale or orca is a toothed whale belonging to the oceanic dolphin family, of which it is the largest member. Killer whales are found in all oceans, from Arctic and Antarctic regions to tropical seas.
